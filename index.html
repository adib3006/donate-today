Our Donate Plan

Here is the sectors we help with your donations. We try to provide the needed people the most necessary things in their sufferings.

Food for a family

We provide emergency food support in areas affected in natural calamities of place of crisises. Food such as dried foods, dried nuts, medicines etc which will last them for almost a week. With your generous help we try to bring smile in hungry faces.

Shelter for a family

In the times of crisis, people face unbearable sufferings with their family. The shelter centers usually can't provide support to 100% people. So, we have taken an initiative to provide temporary shelters and locations around the affected area where they receive food and medical support also.

Education for a child

Your help doesn't only support people in natural or man-made disasters, it also supports child education in rural areas as well as city slums. Here our vo;unteers provide free education with small financial support based on their necessity. This in return ensures a proper opportunity for the most needed.
